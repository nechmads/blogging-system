# Notifications

Hot Metal sends email notifications to keep you informed about important events -- new content ideas, drafts that are ready, published posts, and reader comments. You can turn each notification type on or off individually.

## Managing your preferences

1. Go to **Settings** and scroll down to the **Notifications** section.
2. You will see a toggle switch for each notification type.
3. Click a toggle to turn that notification on or off. Changes save immediately.

## Notification types

| Notification | What it means |
|---|---|
| **New ideas found** | The Content Scout has discovered new content ideas for one of your publications. |
| **Draft ready for review** | An auto-generated draft has been written and is waiting for you to review it. |
| **Post auto-published** | A post has been automatically published to your blog. |
| **New comment** | A reader left a comment on one of your published posts. |

## Defaults

All notification types are enabled by default when you create your account. You can disable any of them at any time.

## Delivery

All notifications are delivered by email to the address associated with your Hot Metal account. There are no in-app notifications at this time.
