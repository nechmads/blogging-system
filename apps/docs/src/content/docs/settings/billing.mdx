# Plans & Billing

Hot Metal offers three plans so you can pick the level that fits your content workflow. Start free, and upgrade whenever you need more capacity.

## Plans overview

### Creator (free)

The Creator plan is free forever -- no credit card required. It includes everything you need to start publishing:

- **2 publications**
- **3 topics** per publication
- **3 auto-published posts** per week
- Built-in (prebuilt) writing styles
- Unlimited AI writing sessions
- Unlimited social publishing
- AI image generation
- All templates
- RSS/Atom feeds
- Reader comments

### Growth (paid)

The Growth plan is for creators who are serious about growing their audience consistently. It includes everything in Creator, plus:

- **5 publications**
- **Unlimited topics** per publication
- **10 auto-published posts** per week
- All Content Scout schedules, including multiple times per day
- Up to **5 custom writing styles**
- Priority support

Growth is available on a monthly or yearly billing cycle. The yearly option gives you a discount compared to paying month-by-month.

### Enterprise (custom pricing)

Enterprise is built for teams and organizations with advanced needs. It includes everything in Growth, plus:

- Unlimited publications
- Unlimited posts per week
- Unlimited writing styles
- Custom domain (planned)
- Team collaboration (planned)
- Custom templates (planned)
- API access (planned)
- SSO (planned)
- Dedicated support and SLA

To learn more about Enterprise pricing, contact us at **hello@hotmetalapp.com**.

## Upgrading to Growth

You can upgrade from Creator to Growth in two ways:

1. **From Settings** -- Go to **Settings > Plan & Billing** and click **Upgrade to Growth**.
2. **From an upgrade prompt** -- Whenever you hit a Creator-tier limit (for example, trying to create a fourth topic), Hot Metal will show an upgrade prompt. Click **Upgrade** to continue.

Either path opens the pricing view, where you can toggle between **Monthly** and **Yearly** billing. When you're ready, click **Start Growth Plan** and complete checkout through Paddle. Your upgrade takes effect immediately.

## Managing your billing

Once you have an active Growth subscription, go to **Settings > Plan & Billing**. You will see your current plan name, status badge, and next billing date.

Click **Manage Billing** to open the Paddle customer portal, where you can:

- Update your payment method
- View and download invoices
- Change your billing address

Paddle handles all billing, invoicing, and tax compliance. It accepts major credit and debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and various local payment methods depending on your region.

## Plan status badges

Your plan card in Settings displays a status badge so you always know where things stand:

| Badge | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Active** | Your subscription is current and in good standing. |
| **Trial** | You are on a free trial of the Growth plan. |
| **Canceled** | You canceled, but you still have access until the end of your current billing period. The badge shows the exact date your access ends. |
| **Past Due** | A payment failed. Update your payment method in the Paddle portal to avoid interruption. |
| **Paused** | Your subscription is paused. Resume it from the Paddle portal. |

## Canceling your subscription

If you decide Growth is not for you, go to **Settings > Plan & Billing** and click **Cancel Plan**. A confirmation modal will explain what happens next:

- Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period.
- After that, your account reverts to the free Creator plan.
- Your content is never deleted, but some features may be limited by the Creator-tier caps (for example, only 3 topics per publication).

Click **Cancel Plan** in the modal to confirm, or **Keep Plan** if you change your mind.
