# Quick Start

Hot Metal is an AI-powered content platform that helps you create, manage, and publish blog posts with the help of AI. It discovers trending topics in your niche, generates content ideas, drafts posts through a conversational AI writing partner, and publishes finished pieces to your blog and social media channels -- all from one place.

## Create your account

To get started, head to the Hot Metal sign-up page and create an account. You can sign up with your email address, or use your existing Google or GitHub account for one-click registration.

Once you've signed up, you'll land on the **Dashboard** -- your home base for everything in Hot Metal.

## Your first visit

If this is your first time signing in, the Dashboard will greet you by name and present the **Getting Started checklist**. This guided walkthrough takes you from zero to your first published post in six steps. We recommend following it in order -- each step builds on the previous one.

You can learn more about the checklist in the [Onboarding Checklist](/getting-started/onboarding-checklist) guide.

## Navigating Hot Metal

The sidebar gives you access to every section of the platform:

- **Dashboard** -- Your home base. See your publications, latest ideas, and active writing sessions at a glance.
- **Publications** -- Create and manage your blogs. Each publication has its own topics, ideas, and published posts.
- **Ideas** -- Browse content ideas generated by the AI-powered Content Scout. Review, promote, or dismiss ideas as you see fit.
- **Writing** -- Start AI-assisted writing sessions where you collaborate with an AI partner to draft posts through conversation.
- **Styles** -- Define custom writing styles so the AI matches your voice, tone, and formatting preferences.
- **Settings** -- Manage your account, connected services, and subscription.
